Complete Product Overview — Final Review

What We Are Building
A fully automated, AI-powered personalized affirmation platform sold to communities. Every user receives a completely custom 21-day progressive journey — built around their specific problem, goal, and life situation — delivered as daily videos, audio, and infographics with an AI coaching experience running alongside it.
The fundamental difference from anything existing: content is not generic. Every user's affirmations, audio, and coaching are built for them specifically. No two users get the same journey.
Reference product is auraloop.in — fixed generic content for fixed themes. We deliver what they deliver but personalized, progressive, and interactive.

Who It Is For
Individual users across multiple life tracks. Primary market is India. Sold to communities. Each user's journey is entirely private — not social, not shared with other community members.

Life Tracks
User picks one track at sign-up: Wealth, Health, Career, Confidence, Relationships, Peace, Fitness, and others. The track sets the visual theme and the framing of the affirmation journey. The content within the track is completely personalized to that individual's situation.

The Complete User Journey

Before Sign-Up — Landing Page
User arrives at the product page and sees a pre-made sample video and infographic. Not personalized, but high quality — demonstrating the visual style, audio quality, and format. This is the product demo. One-time production effort, shown to all visitors.

Onboarding — Free, Approximately 10 Minutes
Step 1 — Pick a life track

User selects from the available tracks.
Step 2 — Two open questions

User writes freely in their own words:

"What's one thing in this area of your life that feels stuck or not moving the way you want?"
"If this was fully resolved a year from now, what would your life feel or look like?"

Step 3 — AI surfaces the hidden layer

Based on both answers, the AI generates what it believes are the three things the user typically cannot articulate themselves: what their inner voice is telling them about why it is hard, what identity shift is needed, and what single belief needs to change. These are presented as suggestions — not questions. User confirms or edits each one. This gives people a skeleton to react to rather than a blank page to fill.
Step 4 — Affirmation calibration

AI generates a 4-point preview: Day 1, Day 7, Day 14, Day 21. User answers two questions: "Does Day 1 feel believable today?" and "Does Day 21 feel like an honest but inspiring stretch?" AI calibrates the intensity of the full 21-day arc based on the response. User confirms. The complete 21-day affirmation set is generated and locked. No need to review all 21 individually.
Step 5 — Content preferences

User selects:

Language: Hindi, English, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, and other major Indian languages
Background music style: from a preset library — calm, uplifting, meditative, energetic, and others

Step 6 — The conversion moment (before payment)

Two things are shown to the user at zero cost before any payment:

Their Day 1 infographic, fully generated — their actual personalized content, their words, on a real card
A 15–20 second audio preview from the first line of their Day 1 affirmation in their chosen language with music

This is the moment the user realizes the product is genuinely built for them. Payment prompt appears here.

Content Generation — Fully Automated, Runs After Payment
For each of the 21 days, the system generates:
Morning video — AI-spoken affirmation in energizing tone, chosen background music, stage-matched visual background, affirmation text displayed on screen. Approximately 2–3 minutes.
Evening video — Same affirmation in calming tone, softer music, deeper visual mood. Approximately 2–3 minutes.
Infographic — Static visual card showing the day's Doubt → Reframe → Truth → Action. For quick daily reference.
Total per user: 42 videos + 21 infographics.
On the video visuals — stage-progressive library:

Videos use pre-built visual backgrounds organized by life track and journey stage. The four stages map to the 21 days naturally:

Days 1–5 (Identity): quiet, introspective visuals — seed, dawn, stillness
Days 6–10 (Clarity): light, focus, direction — something coming into view
Days 11–15 (Creation): movement, energy, building — things taking shape
Days 16–21 (Launch & Growth): expansion, abundance, achievement

Within each stage, 3 visual variations rotate across the days so consecutive days feel different. Morning and evening use different visual moods within the same stage. Affirmation text on screen changes every day regardless.
Per life track: 4 stages × 3 variations × 2 moods = 24 video backgrounds. Across 7 tracks: 168 backgrounds total. One-time production effort serving unlimited users.

The 21-Day Daily Journey
Two counters run simultaneously for every user:
Calendar Days — real days elapsed since the user started. Increments automatically every real day.
Affirmation Days — which affirmation day the user is currently on, 1 through 21. Only increments when the previous day is fully completed.
These can diverge. If a user started 12 days ago but only completed 8 affirmation days, their dashboard shows Calendar Day 12, Affirmation Day 8. At the end of their journey they see: "You completed 21 affirmation days in X calendar days." Honest mirror, no punishment, full accountability.
The unlock chain within each day:

Morning video watched → Evening video unlocked → Evening video watched + daily check-in completed → Next affirmation day unlocked.
If morning video is not watched, evening video stays locked. Every step must be completed in sequence.
Daily flow:

Morning notification → User opens app → Watches morning video → Views infographic → Evening video is now available. Evening notification → Watches evening video → Completes daily check-in → Next day unlocks → Progress card generated.
Daily check-in questions:

How true does today's doubt feel right now? (1–10)
How believable does the response feel? (1–10)
How much resistance did you feel today? (1–10)
How strongly did you act like this new identity today? (1–10)
Did you complete your action? (Yes / Partially / No)
Optional free text: what evidence did you create today?

The system records the exact timestamp of when morning and evening videos were watched each day.
AI conversational coach — available anytime:

User can type any doubt or message at any point during the journey. The AI responds with a personalized reframe using that day's dialogue structure, adapted to their exact words. This is the proactive delivery plus reactive coaching layer — the "agentic" dimension.
Progress card:

Auto-generated when a day is fully completed. Shown in-app and shareable — user can download and send to WhatsApp, Instagram Stories, or anywhere else.

Progress Dashboard
User can see at any time:

Calendar Days vs Affirmation Days — the two counters, shown together
Doubt score trend — should move downward over days (core proof of impact)
Believability score trend — should move upward over days
Resistance score over time
Identity score over time
Action completion rate across all completed days (Yes / Partially / No breakdown)
Consistency rate — percentage of calendar days where an affirmation day was completed
Identity gap closing — visual showing how believability on Day 1 compares to today
Streak — consecutive affirmation days without a gap
Momentum index — composite weekly score across all four metrics
Timestamps of morning and evening video watches for each completed day


Day 21 — Completion and Renewal
User sees their full journey summary: total calendar days taken, how doubt score changed from start to finish, action completion rate, consistency rate, and overall momentum.
21-in-21 Achievement:

If calendar days equal affirmation days equal 21 — meaning the user completed the journey with perfect daily consistency — they earn a special badge and a renewal discount. The badge is shareable as a card. This is a recognition of genuine discipline and a viral moment.
Video feedback:

After Day 21, the app prompts the user to record a 30–60 second video about what changed during their journey. On submission they receive an additional discount on their next cycle. The video stays in the product's testimonial library with their permission.
Renewal options:

Same goal, deeper conditioning — ₹699 Standard / ₹1,299 Premium
New goal, fresh onboarding — full price ₹999 Standard / ₹1,999 Premium
21-in-21 discount applies on top
