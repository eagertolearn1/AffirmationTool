## Product Vision

An AI-powered identity change platform for communities. The product takes each user's specific situation — their real problem, their goal, and their inner blocks — and builds a completely personalized 21-day progressive journey to close the gap between who they are today and who they need to become.

Affirmations, audio, and visual content are the delivery mechanism. Identity change is the outcome.

This is not an affirmation app. The same engine applies across all major life domains and opens future opportunities in corporate wellness, leadership development, habit change, financial behavior coaching, and more.

Every user's journey is unique. No two people receive the same content. The product is personalized by AI with built-in safety and user control. Fully scalable.

---

## Scientific Credibility Framework

When users or partners ask why this works, the product is positioned as combining five established behavioral science disciplines:

**Cognitive Reframing** — The daily Doubt → Reframe → Truth structure directly applies cognitive reframing principles. Users are not suppressing doubt; they are actively replacing it with an evidence-based counter-narrative.

**Identity-Based Behavior Change** — Drawn from James Clear's framework: behavior change that lasts requires an identity shift, not just habit formation. The product targets the belief layer ("I am someone who...") rather than the action layer ("I will do...").

**Self-Efficacy Theory (Bandura)** — The 21-day progressive arc is calibrated to the user's current believability level. Starting with achievable Day 1 affirmations and building to stretch Day 21 affirmations mirrors mastery experiences, the strongest driver of self-efficacy.

**Habit Formation** — Morning and evening structure creates two daily cues tied to existing routines. Consistency tracking and streaks reinforce the habit loop.

**Reflective Journaling** — Daily check-ins, free-text evidence entries, and AI coaching conversations serve the same function as structured journaling: metacognitive awareness of change as it happens.

The word "affirmations" is used in marketing and in the product. The methodology behind it is positioned using this framework in the app's About section, in any B2B or institutional context, and whenever scientific credibility is relevant.

## Safety and Guardrails — Built Into Every Layer

### Crisis and Clinical Safety

The system detects crisis signals in all user-entered text across onboarding, check-ins, and AI coaching — signals of suicide, self-harm, abuse, and severe distress. When detected, the normal product flow stops immediately. The AI responds with warmth and human acknowledgement, provides iCall (9152987821) and Vandrevala Foundation (1860-2662-345), and never delivers an affirmation in that moment. This is non-negotiable and built into the core AI prompt layer.

If a user's onboarding answer describes a diagnosed clinical condition — depression, PTSD, bipolar disorder, eating disorder — the system acknowledges it, recommends professional support, and gives the user a clear choice to continue or not. It never silently proceeds.

If a user describes extreme financial distress or crisis, relevant financial counselling resources are provided alongside continued support.

### Legal and Positioning

The product never describes itself as therapy, treatment, counselling, or a mental health service — not on the landing page, not in the app, not in any notification.

Clear disclaimer at sign-up, on the landing page, and in settings at all times: "This product is for personal growth and self-development. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care."

No medical outcome claims in marketing. "Helps you feel more confident" is acceptable. "Treats anxiety" is not. All marketing language reviewed against this boundary before publishing.

Age gate: users must be 18 or above to sign up.

### AI and Content Safety

Users always know they are interacting with AI, not a human coach. Clear disclosure in the coaching interface.

Every affirmation generated by the AI passes through automated content moderation before being stored or shown to the user. Raw AI output is never directly delivered.

Cultural sensitivity flag available — users can report any affirmation that feels culturally or personally inappropriate. This triggers regeneration.

No religious or spiritual content included unless the user has explicitly opted in.

No manipulative framing anywhere in the product — content is empowering and identity-building, never fear-based or dependency-creating.

Human support channel visible in the app — users can reach a support contact when something feels wrong.

### Data and Privacy

Full compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA). Users can access, correct, and delete all their personal data at any time through settings.

Voice samples for Premium users processed by third-party APIs — explicit written consent required before recording begins. Users are told which service processes their voice data, how long it is retained, and how to request deletion.

All progress data, check-in answers, and free text entries are sensitive personal data. Encrypted at rest and in transit.

Explicit consent required before any user's video feedback is used as a testimonial.

### Dependency and Wellbeing

If a user sends an unusually high volume of AI coaching messages in a short period — a signal of possible distress or unhealthy reliance — the AI gently suggests speaking to someone they trust, while continuing to support them. (Note: coaching limits also serve as a structural safeguard here — see AI Coaching Limits section.)

Clear in-app messaging throughout: the AI coach is a structured growth tool, not a substitute for human relationships or professional support.

### Operational Safety

All crisis detections logged anonymously for ongoing product safety review.

Clear internal process for handling user reports of harmful product experiences.

Terms of service and privacy policy in plain language, available in Hindi and English from launch.

### Sign-Up

User provides name, email, and WhatsApp number.

WhatsApp communication is **explicit opt-in** — an unchecked checkbox the user must actively select:

> ☐ Send me daily reminders and journey updates on WhatsApp

No pre-selected consent. Users who opt in receive WhatsApp communications. Users who don't can always enable it later from settings. Every WhatsApp message includes "Reply STOP to opt out."

Age gate confirmed: 18 and above only.

---

### Onboarding — Free, Target Under 7 Minutes, Under 20 Interactions

Visible progress bar throughout. Auto-saves at every step. Skip-and-return option at Step 3.

**Step 1 — Pick a life track**
One tap. 10 seconds.

**Step 2 — Two open questions**
User answers in their own words. Example prompts shown to help users articulate their situation.
- "What's one thing in this area of your life that feels stuck or not moving the way you want?"
- "If this was fully resolved a year from now, what would your life feel or look like?"

**Step 3 — AI surfaces the hidden layer**
Based on both answers, the AI generates: what the inner voice is saying about why change is hard, what identity shift is needed, and what single belief needs to change. These appear as suggestions the user confirms or edits — not blank questions.

**Step 4 — Affirmation calibration**
AI generates a 4-point preview: Day 1, Day 7, Day 14, Day 21.
- "Does Day 1 feel believable today?" (Yes / Slightly too big / Way too big)
- "Does Day 21 feel like an honest and inspiring stretch?" (Yes / Too small / Too big)

AI calibrates the full 21-day arc. User confirms.

**Step 5 — Content preferences**
- Language: Hindi, English, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam
- Background music style: calm, uplifting, meditative, energetic

**Step 6 — The conversion moment (before payment)**
Two things generated specifically for this user at no cost:
- Their Day 1 infographic — actual personalized content on a real card
- A 15–20 second audio preview of the first line of their Day 1 affirmation

This is the moment personalization becomes real. Payment prompt appears here.

### The 21-Day Daily Journey

#### Two Counters

**Calendar Days** — real days elapsed since starting. Auto-increments daily.

**Affirmation Days** — which affirmation day the user is on. Only increments on full completion of the previous day.

A user who started 14 days ago but completed only 9 affirmation days sees: Calendar Day 14 / Affirmation Day 9. Day 21 completion shows: "You completed 21 affirmation days in X calendar days."

#### Unlock Chain

Morning audio must be listened to before evening audio unlocks. Evening audio must be listened to before the check-in becomes available. Once both audio sessions and the check-in are complete, the next affirmation day unlocks. If the previous day's check-in is not completed, the next day auto-unlocks after 24 real hours — check-in drives Transformation Score and gamification rewards, not access.

#### Daily Screen Experience (Phase 1)

Beautiful static background image matched to life track and stage. Infographic card displayed prominently. The day's Truth statement shown as synchronized text that highlights as the audio speaks it. Audio player controls at the bottom. Morning and evening follow the same structure with different audio and tonal treatment.

#### Daily Check-In — Designed for Fatigue Prevention

**Milestone days only (1, 7, 14, 21) — full check-in:**
- How true does today's doubt feel right now? (1–10)
- How believable does the response feel? (1–10)
- How much resistance did you feel today? (1–10)
- How strongly did you act like this new identity today? (1–10)
- Did you complete your action? (Yes / Partially / No)
- Optional free text: what evidence did you create today?

**All other days — 30-second check-in:**
- Believability score (1–10) — always present, most important metric
- One rotating question from the remaining set

#### AI Conversational Coach

Available anytime during the journey. User types any doubt or message. AI responds with a personalized reframe using that day's dialogue structure, adapted to the user's exact words. Has context of the user's specific problem, identity statement, and current stage. Coaching history stored per journey.

**Usage limits:** Standard 5 interactions/day · Premium 20 interactions/day · Additional credits purchasable

#### WhatsApp Communications (Opt-In Users)

- Morning reminder with deep link at user's preferred time
- Evening reminder with deep link
- Re-engagement nudge if inactive for 48 hours — sent once, not repeatedly
- Day completion congratulations with progress card attached
- Milestone messages on Days 7, 14, and 21
- Achievement notifications with badge cards

#### Telegram (Optional, User-Initiated)

- Community broadcast channel for motivation, tips, and success stories
- Optional bot for AI coaching conversations — same engine as in-app, same daily limits

#### Progress Card

Auto-generated when a day is fully completed. Shows user's name, affirmation day, calendar day, the day's Truth statement, and current Transformation Score. Shareable — downloadable for WhatsApp, Instagram Stories, or anywhere.

### Achievement System — Five Badges

Multiple achievement types prevent discouragement and reward different behaviors:

| Badge | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Journey Completer** | Completes all 21 affirmation days — baseline, anyone can earn this |
| **Perfect Consistency** | Calendar days = Affirmation days = 21 — no gaps ever |
| **Strong Momentum** | Completes Days 1–14 without a single gap |
| **Comeback Champion** | Resumes after a 3+ day gap and still completes the full journey |
| **Action Taker** | 80% or higher action completion rate across all milestone check-ins |

All badges are shareable as image cards. Perfect Consistency and Comeback Champion both carry renewal discounts. Journey Completer is the universal minimum — every user who finishes can celebrate.

---

### Transformation Score — The Headline Metric

A single number from 0 to 100 moving across the 21-day journey. Calculated from four weighted inputs: consistency rate (highest weight), believability trend, doubt reduction trend, and action completion rate.

Dashboard headline, progress card centerpiece, Day 21 story, and renewal conversation anchor.

---

### Progress Dashboard

- Calendar Days vs Affirmation Days
- Transformation Score — current number and trend over time
- Doubt score trend — moving downward
- Believability score trend — moving upward
- Resistance and identity scores over time
- Action completion breakdown
- Consistency rate — percentage of calendar days with a completed affirmation day
- Streak — consecutive affirmation days without a gap
- Identity gap closing — Day 1 believability vs today
- Morning and evening listen timestamps per day

---

### Day 21 — Completion and Renewal

Full journey summary: calendar days taken, Transformation Score arc from Day 1 to Day 21, action completion rate, consistency rate.

**Video feedback:** User records a 30–60 second video about what changed. On submission, a discount is applied to next cycle. Video goes to testimonial library with explicit permission.

**Renewal options:**
- Same goal, deeper conditioning — ₹699 Standard / ₹1,299 Premium
- New goal, fresh onboarding — full price ₹999 Standard / ₹1,999 Premium
- Achievement-based discounts (Perfect Consistency, Comeback Champion) applied on top
- Video feedback discount applied on top

## Phase 1 vs Phase 2

### Phase 1 — Launch

Complete product as described. Audio + infographic + affirmation text synchronized display. Beautiful static background image. No video generation pipeline.

Deliverables per user: 42 audio files + 21 infographics.

### Phase 2 — After Launch Validation

Static background and audio experience replaced with composed morning and evening videos. Pre-built background video library of 168 videos generated once (7 tracks × 4 stages × 3 variations × 2 moods). Video composition API combines background video, audio, and affirmation text overlay. The audio player becomes a video player. All other product features identical.
