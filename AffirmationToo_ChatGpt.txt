That's exactly the right approach.

When your mind says, "This isn't true," the problem isn't your ambition—it's the gap between your current identity and your desired identity.

Instead of using affirmations, you'll use affirmation dialogues.

The structure is:

Fear → Acknowledge → Reframe → Action

Your conscious mind raises a concern. Your wiser self responds with evidence, logic, and a small next step.

The goal isn't to silence doubt. The goal is to teach your mind that uncertainty and progress can coexist.
